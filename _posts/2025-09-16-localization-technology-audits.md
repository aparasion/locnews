- The rapid evolution of AI and automation is transforming localization operations, necessitating urgent technology upgrades for global companies to remain competitive.
- Localization technology audits are essential for assessing and optimizing the technology stacks that support localization processes, identifying inefficiencies, and recommending improvements.
- These audits focus on tools like translation management systems (TMS) and AI-driven solutions, contrasting with broader program audits that evaluate overall strategy and processes.
- Regular technology audits help organizations streamline their systems, reduce costs, and enhance quality, ultimately impacting their ability to scale globally.
- As AI becomes integral to localization, companies must proactively evaluate their tech stacks to leverage new efficiencies and maintain a competitive edge in the market.
