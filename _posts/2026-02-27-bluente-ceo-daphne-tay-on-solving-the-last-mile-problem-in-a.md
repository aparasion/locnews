Daphne Tay, CEO of Bluente, discusses the challenges of last-mile AI document translation in a recent interview. She emphasizes the importance of accuracy and cultural context in translations, which are often overlooked by automated systems. Tay highlights that while AI can handle large volumes of text, human oversight is essential to ensure nuanced understanding and relevance. Bluente aims to bridge this gap by combining AI technology with expert human translators, thereby enhancing the quality of translations for businesses operating in diverse markets. The implications of this approach could lead to more effective communication and reduced misunderstandings in international dealings, ultimately benefiting global commerce.
