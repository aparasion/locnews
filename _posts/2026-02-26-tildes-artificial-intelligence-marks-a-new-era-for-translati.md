- Tilde, a Latvian language technology company, has launched TildeOpen LLM, a machine translation platform offering high-quality translations in 34 European languages, now accessible to the public and enterprises.
- The model, significantly smaller than competitors like ChatGPT-4.1, delivers competitive translation quality while ensuring data privacy, as it can be deployed on-premises or in Europe-based clouds.
- TildeOpen was developed with support from the European Commission and trained on extensive datasets, making it a robust tool for both private and public sector translation needs.
- The platform reduces post-editing workload and allows for customization to meet specific user requirements, enhancing its practicality for everyday use.
- Tilde aims to make high-quality language technology reliable and accessible, marking a significant advancement in AI applications for European languages.
