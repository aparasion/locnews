The Zurich University of Applied Sciences (ZHAW) is offering a series of eight online webinars in 2026 aimed at professionals in the language industry. These sessions will cover key topics such as advancements in artificial intelligence, consultancy skills for language professionals, and the integration of innovative tools to enhance productivity. Each 30-minute session will include a Q&A segment, providing participants with the opportunity to engage directly with experts. The first webinar is set for April 17, with subsequent sessions running until December 18. This initiative presents a valuable opportunity for language professionals to stay updated on industry trends and enhance their skills.
