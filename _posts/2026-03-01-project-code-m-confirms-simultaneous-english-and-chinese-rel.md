Project Code M has officially announced that it will release its content simultaneously in English and Chinese, catering to a broader audience. This decision highlights the project's commitment to inclusivity and accessibility in the gaming community. Additionally, NOVECT, the studio behind the project, has rejected the use of AI translation, emphasizing the importance of human touch in localization. This stance suggests a dedication to maintaining cultural nuances and quality in the game's dialogue and narrative. The implications of these choices could set a precedent for future projects in the industry, potentially influencing how developers approach localization and audience engagement in a global market.
