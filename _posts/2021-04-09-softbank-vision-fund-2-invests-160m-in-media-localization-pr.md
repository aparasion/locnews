Iyuno-SDI Group, a media localization services provider, has secured $160 million in funding from SoftBank Vision Fund 2, making the fund one of its largest shareholders. This investment follows Iyuno Media Group's recent acquisition of SDI Media, which has strengthened its position in the industry. The funding will likely enhance Iyuno-SDI's capabilities in providing translated subtitles and other localization services, potentially expanding its market reach and improving service offerings. This move reflects growing demand for media localization as global content consumption increases, positioning Iyuno-SDI for further growth in a competitive landscape.
