The article discusses the transformative impact of artificial intelligence (AI) on the global manga fandom, particularly in translation and accessibility. AI tools are increasingly being used to translate manga, allowing fans worldwide to access content that was previously unavailable in their languages. This shift not only enhances the reading experience for non-Japanese speakers but also fosters a more inclusive community of manga enthusiasts. However, the rise of AI translation raises concerns about the quality and accuracy of translations, as well as the potential loss of cultural nuances. The implications of this technology could reshape how manga is consumed and appreciated, potentially diminishing the role of traditional translators while expanding the reach of manga to a broader audience. Overall, AI is poised to redefine the landscape of manga fandom, balancing accessibility with the preservation of cultural integrity.
