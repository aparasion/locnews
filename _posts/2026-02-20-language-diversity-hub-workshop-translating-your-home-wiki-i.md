- The Language Diversity Hub Workshop focuses on enhancing the accessibility of Wikimedia projects by translating home wiki interfaces into various languages.
- Participants will learn techniques for effective translation and localization, promoting inclusivity in the Wikimedia community.
- The initiative aims to empower non-English speakers and increase participation in content creation across diverse linguistic backgrounds.
- This workshop is part of a broader effort to celebrate and preserve linguistic diversity within the Wikimedia ecosystem.
