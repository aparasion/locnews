- **Company Overview**: Bitvavo is a rapidly growing European fintech firm specializing in crypto and trading, primarily based in the Netherlands but targeting broader European markets with an English-first strategy.

- **Localization Strategy**: The company serves six languages (English, German, Spanish, Dutch, French, and Italian) to cater to key markets including the Netherlands, Germany, France, and Spain. Localization needs are increasing due to rapid growth in product and marketing.

- **Leadership and Approach**: Emma Wallerstein, the Localization Program Manager, emphasizes a consultative role for localization, integrating it across various teams to enhance product development and customer communication.

- **Technological Integration**: Bitvavo utilizes Lokalise TMS for translation management, supplemented by freelancers and machine translation, with plans to scale operations and improve localization maturity.

- **Future Goals**: The company aims to define localization’s role more clearly, enhance tooling, break down silos, and establish a shared localization infrastructure as demand for localization services continues to rise.
