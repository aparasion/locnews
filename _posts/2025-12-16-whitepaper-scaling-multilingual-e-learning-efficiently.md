- The e-learning industry has surged to an estimated USD 250-300 billion, driven by the COVID-19 pandemic and expected to continue growing at double-digit rates.
- Technology platforms are enhancing global reach, emphasizing the importance of delivering courses in learners' native languages for better engagement and retention.
- AI localization is becoming the preferred method for creating scalable, multilingual e-learning content, addressing the challenges of traditional human-only workflows.
- A whitepaper by Laszlo K. Varga and Beata Janaszkiewicz-Cavalcanti, sponsored by Centific, highlights the need for efficient scaling of multilingual e-learning through AI dubbing.
- The research utilized insights from Nimdzi’s expert network and interviews with localization managers, underscoring the growing role of AI in making e-learning truly international.
