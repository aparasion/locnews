- The European Commission is seeking a new Director for the Translation Centre for the Bodies of the EU, with applications due by March 19, 2026.
- The Centre, based in Luxembourg, provides translation services in all 24 EU languages to over 70 clients and employs around 200 staff.
- Key responsibilities for the Director include strategic management, budget oversight, staff leadership, and developing the Centre’s business model in response to industry trends.
- Candidates must be EU citizens with at least five years of high-level management experience, proficiency in one EU language, and knowledge of another, along with familiarity with the language industry and EU administrative procedures.
- The appointment is for a five-year term, renewable, and candidates must complete the term before reaching the retirement age of 66. Applications are submitted online through the Commission's portal.
