Project Code M has officially confirmed that it will be released in English simultaneously with its original version, addressing concerns from international fans. This decision aims to enhance accessibility and engagement for a broader audience. Additionally, NOVECT, the game's developer, has rejected the use of AI translation for localizing the game, emphasizing their commitment to maintaining the integrity and quality of the narrative. This approach reflects a growing trend in the gaming industry to prioritize authentic localization over automated solutions, ensuring that cultural nuances and context are preserved. The simultaneous release and dedication to quality localization could potentially boost the game's reception and sales in English-speaking markets.
